# 🍕 Pizza Sales Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes one year of pizza sales data to uncover customer purchasing patterns, sales trends, and business opportunities. Using **Microsoft Excel**, I performed data cleaning, analysis, KPI tracking, and built an interactive dashboard to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

The pizza restaurant experienced a decline in sales and wanted to identify the key factors affecting revenue, customer behavior, and product performance in order to improve business growth.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Skills Used

* 📊 Microsoft Excel
* 📈 Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts
* 🔍 Data Cleaning & Data Analysis
* 📉 Dashboard Design
* 🧮 Excel Functions (VLOOKUP, COUNT, AVERAGE, MAX, Filters)

---

## 📊 Key KPIs

* 💰 **Total Revenue:** $817,860
* 🛒 **Total Orders:** 21,350
* 🍕 **Total Pizzas Sold:** 49,574
* 💵 **Average Order Value:** $38.31
* 📦 **Average Pizzas per Order:** 2.32

---

## 🔍 Key Business Insights

* 🍕 **Classic** category generated the highest sales and orders.
* 📏 **Large** pizzas contributed the highest revenue.
* 🌶️ **Thai Chicken Pizza** generated the highest revenue.
* ⭐ **Classic Deluxe Pizza** received the highest number of orders.
* ❌ **Brie Carre Pizza** was the least popular and lowest-performing product.
* 🌞 **Summer & Spring** recorded the highest sales, while **Fall** had the lowest.
* ⏰ Peak ordering hours were **12 PM–1 PM** and **5 PM–6 PM**.

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

* Executive KPI Summary
* Sales Trend Analysis
* Category & Size Performance
* Best & Worst Selling Pizzas
* Seasonal Sales Insights
* Interactive Pivot Charts & Filters

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Launch seasonal promotions during low-sales periods.
* Focus marketing on high-performing pizza categories.
* Review or replace underperforming menu items.
* Optimize inventory based on seasonal demand.
